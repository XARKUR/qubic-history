    🤝 Proposal: Appointment of Community Lead as Jg ױ (@jgvks) & Operational Roadmap for Qubic

    👤 Introduction & Personal Background

    Dear Qubic Community and Investors,

    Following the unanimous agreement of the Strategic Board and with the full backing of the Community Team, I am officially presenting my candidacy for the role of Community Lead for Qubic.

    This vital position has been vacant since the departure of our former Community Lead, Elclip, who did an outstanding job, building upon the excellent foundation laid by his mentor, Tom. My goal is to leverage the proven frameworks and successful ideas that have brought us here, while injecting fresh, structured strategies to help Qubic scale safely and efficiently.

    For those who do not know me yet, I have been a deeply committed Qubic investor since August 2023, a voluntary moderator across various Telegram channels, and an official member of the internal team since 2025. Having progressed through the Ambassador Program, I have had the privilege of working closely with and learning from many of Qubic’s historical and current figures, while actively participating in multiple workgroups and targeted ecosystem campaigns.

    I am requesting your official approval today because, viewing the project both as an investor and an insider, I have identified operational bottlenecks that can be corrected. We are privileged to have a Dev Team delivering fabulous work that puts 99% of Web3 projects to shame, a hyperactive marketing team producing stunning content, an elite scientific team gathering distinctions worldwide, and a business team working relentlessly behind the scenes. The only missing piece is a seamless bridge between these departments and our community—a gap I intend to close with the support of our solid and loyal Community Team.

    To ensure this proposal is built on objective data rather than subjectivity, I recently conducted a structured, anonymous diagnostic survey within the team to establish our operational baseline. The data shows a highly resilient team with a healthy psychological threshold of 2.2/5 on the stress/burnout scale (where a lower score represents less stress, 1 being optimal) and an outstanding vision alignment and motivation score of 4.1/5 (where a higher score represents greater alignment, 5 being optimal). While we must acknowledge a potential upward bias inherent in initial snapshots—where the most active and engaged members tend to respond first—this data provides a reliable qualitative foundation. We will systematically account for this potential bias when measuring the real-world velocity of our milestone progression.

    🎯 Strategic Vision & Execution Plan

    If granted your trust, my administration will pivot around three core structural pillars designed to optimize resources and safeguard investor sentiment.

    1️⃣ Streamlining Moderation & Empowering Talent

    We will cut down administrative bureaucracy to allow team members with high-level communication skills to spend more time engaging with users. We will shift from rigid micro-management to trusting the proven judgment of our moderators, while maintaining absolute operational transparency.

    2️⃣ Fixing the Information Flow

    Communication is currently our missing link. This does not refer to marketing—which is executing flawlessly—but rather to internal information routing and the translation of complex technical developments into digestible insights for the everyday investor. A well-informed community that understands core architectural concepts and the realities of development timelines is a community immune to anxiety, panic, and orchestrated FUD campaigns. The Community Team will establish structured touchpoints with every core department to route data efficiently without interrupting the developers' focus.

    3️⃣ Structuring Regional Hubs & Talent Recognition

    We must highlight and formally recognize the regional moderators who have spent years working voluntarily out of pure passion for Qubic. Often isolated in their local sectors, they continue to protect and onboard users globally. We will train, empower, and integrate them into official workgroups. By providing them with structural visibility and professional backing today, we are cultivating the global ecosystem Leads of tomorrow.

    🗺️ Operational Roadmap (Phase-Based Approach)

    To avoid the pitfalls of arbitrary deadlines—which are inherently unpredictable in complex Web3 environments—this roadmap is structured around sequential operational phases with compressed, high-impact timeframes relative to my appointment. Achieving the Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) of one phase automatically triggers the deployment of the next.

    🔹 Phase 1: Operational Infrastructure & Technical Access (Weeks 1–2)

    The immediate priority is to resolve internal technical limitations and secure operational autonomy for the frontline team.

    • Core Objectives:
      • Secure administrative tools and native Discord permissions via admins to eliminate dependencies on the Core Dev team for routine security management.
      • Restructure shift rotations and internal support channels to optimize coverage across all major global time zones.
    • Phase 1 KPIs:
      • Tooling & Autonomy Score: Elevate the team’s current tool adequacy perception score from a critical 2.62/5 to a minimum of 4.0/5 through proper permission allocation.
      • Team Retention: Maintain the current 4.1/5 motivation baseline while ensuring the burnout index remains securely below 2.5/5.
    🔹 Phase 2: The Technical Translation Bridge & FUD Mitigation (Months 1–2)

    With internal tools secured, the team immediately pivots to resolving external communication friction and protecting investor sentiment.

    • Core Objectives:
      • Launch a "Technical Translation" framework: converting complex GitHub updates, node data, and protocol milestones into clear, weekly community summaries.
      • Establish an internal SLA (Service Level Agreement) with the Business and Dev teams to receive official talking points on critical updates before they become a source of community speculation.
    • Phase 2 KPIs:
      • Response Latency: Maintain an average response time of under 15 minutes for verified user inquiries in primary support channels, establishing a clear pathway to optimize this down to under 5 minutes as workflow automations mature.
      • Sentiment Stability: A measurable reduction in repetitive chat anxiety regarding technical milestones, verified via qualitative weekly sentiment reports presented to the Board.
    🔹 Phase 3: Regional Hub Integration & Talent Recognition (Month 3 & Beyond)

    Focuses on long-term sustainability, expanding Qubic's global reach, and highlighting core contributors.

    • Core Objectives:
      • Establish a formal recognition and value matrix for long-standing voluntary regional contributors (including custom roles, dedicated profile badges, and direct developer communication channels).
      • Standardize onboarding and training kits across all regional sub-communities to ensure unified messaging and compliance with Qubic's core values.
    • Phase 3 KPIs:
      • Regional Integration: Successfully transition a minimum of [X] local community hubs into the unified operational framework.
      • Recognition Transparency: 100% deployment accuracy of non-monetary recognition milestones, tracked transparently on GitHub/Forum monthly.

    Funding and formalizing the Community Lead position is an investment in Qubic's most defensive asset: its community. This strategy guarantees operational safety, structural transparency, and professional growth management as we continue to scale.

    I welcome your feedback, questions, and insights below before moving this proposal to an official vote.

    Thank you for your trust and dedication to Qubic.

    👤 引言与个人背景

    尊敬的 Qubic 社区成员与投资者：

    在战略委员会（Strategic Board）的一致同意以及社区团队（Community Team）的全力支持下，我正式向大家提交申请，竞选 Qubic 的**社区负责人（Community Lead）**一职。

    自前任社区负责人 Elclip 离职后，这一核心职位一直空缺。Elclip 以及他的导师 Tom 曾开展了极其出色的工作，并奠定了坚实的运营基础。我的目标 es 充分利用这些已被验证的高效工作框架与优秀成果，同时注入全新、结构化的战略，以帮助 Qubic 安全且高效地进行全球化扩展。

    对于还不认识我的朋友，我是自 2023 年 8 月加入的一名深度 Qubic 投资者，曾长期担任多个 Telegram 官方频道的志愿管理员，并于 2025 年正式加入内部团队。在通过大使计划（Ambassador Program）的发展过程中，我有幸与多位 Qubic 历史及现任的核心成员紧密合作并向他们学习，同时积极参与了多个专项工作组和生态目标战役。

    我今天之所以正式提出这份提案，是因为站在投资者和社区团队成员的双重内部视角来看，我发现了当前运营中可以被修正的瓶颈。我们非常幸运地拥有一个执行力极强、让 99% 的 Web3 项目都相形见绌的开发团队（Dev Team），一个产出惊人且内容极具冲击力的营销团队，一个在全球屡获殊荣的顶尖科学团队，以及一个在幕后默默无闻、全天候运转的商务团队。当前唯一的短板，就是这些核心部门与社区之间缺乏一座无缝连接的桥梁——而我，计划在稳固且忠诚 de 社区团队的支持下，彻底补齐这一环。

    为了确保本提案建立在客观数据而非主观臆断之上，我近期在团队内部进行了一次结构化的匿名诊断调查，以此确立我们的运营基准。数据表明，目前我们的团队具备极强的韧性：在压力与倦怠维度（分值越低代表压力越小，1为最佳状态），团队的心理健康分值为 2.2/5；在愿景认同与动力维度（分值越高代表认同度越高，5为最佳状态），团队给出了 4.1/5 的卓越高分。这表明我们的底层团队整体非常健康、极具忠诚度，并已完全做好准备，为 Qubic 重启高质高效的社区运营。

    当然，我们必须理性地承认，在首期数据快照中可能存在一定的“向上偏差（upward bias）”——因为通常最活跃、参与度最高的核心成员往往会最先响应调查。因此，尽管当前的动力指标非常优秀，我们在推进路线图各里程碑时的“实际执行速度”仍将保持审慎与务实，并系统性地将这一潜在偏差考虑在内。

    🎯 战略愿景与执行计划

    如果能获得大家的信任，我的管理工作将围绕以下三个核心结构支柱展开，以优化团队资源并保护投资者的情绪面：

    1️⃣ 精简管理治理与赋能核心成员

    我们将大幅削减繁琐的行政官僚流程，让具备高水平沟通能力的团队成员有更多时间直接投入到与用户的深度互动中。我们将从死板的微观管理转向充分信任我们已被证实的管理员的优秀判断力，同时保持绝对的运营透明度。

    2️⃣ 修复核心信息流

    信息沟通是目前项目整体链路中最脆弱的一环。这指的并不是执行得无懈可击的市场营销，而是指内部技术进展向社区传导的机制，以及如何将复杂的架构开发转化为普通投资者能够轻松理解的直观见解。一个能够及时获取核心信息、理解技术概念和开发周期客观延期的社区，才能天然免疫焦虑、恐慌以及各种有组织、恶意的 FUD 围剿。社区团队将与各个核心部门建立结构化的常态化对接，在不打扰开发人员核心精力的前提下，高效传导一手数据。

    3️⃣ 构建区域枢纽与人才激励认可

    我们必须高度重视并正式认可那些多年来纯粹出于对 Qubic 的热爱而进行无偿志愿工作的区域管理员。他们往往孤军奋战在各自的本地语区，却持续在全球范围内保护并帮助新用户入圈。我们将对其进行培训、赋能，并将其正式吸纳进官方工作组。通过在今天给予 them 系统性的架构能见度与专业支持，我们实际上是在为整个项目的全球生态培育明天的领袖。

    🗺️ 运营路线图（基于阶段的推进模式）

    为了避免陷入盲目设定死板截止日期的陷阱（这在复杂的 Web3 开发环境中本身就是极具不可预测性的），本路线图围绕紧凑、高影响力的连续运营阶段展开，所有时间节点均与我的正式任命时间挂钩。某一阶段的关键绩效指标（KPI）达成后，将自动触发下一阶段的正式部署。

    🔹 阶段 1：运营基础设施与技术权限获取（第 1–2 周）

    当前最核心的紧迫任务是彻底解决首期团队审计中发现的内部技术限制，为一线团队争取运营自主权。

    • 核心目标：
        • 通过管理员落实管理工具及 Discord 原生管理权限的配置，确保社区负责人及核心管理层在处理日常安全事务时，不再依赖或分走核心开发团队（Core Dev）的精力。
      • 重新梳理排班轮换机制与内部技术支持频道，彻底优化全球各大主流时区的全天候覆盖能力。
    • 阶段 1 关键绩效指标（KPIs）：
      • 工具与自主权得分： 通过合理分配系统权限，将团队目前对工具完备度的感知评分从极度危险的 2.62/5 提升至最低 4.0/5。
      • 团队留存率： 维持目前 4.1/5 的高动力基准，同时确保团队整体倦怠指数稳定在 2.5/5 的健康值以下。
    🔹 阶段 2：技术转化桥梁与 FUD 缓解（第 1–2 个月）

    在内部工具链与权限交接稳固后，团队将立即调转矛头，解决外部面向投资者的信息摩擦，全力捍卫投资者信心。

    • 核心目标：
      • 启动“技术翻译（Technical Translation）”常态化框架：将复杂的 GitHub 代码库更新、节点数据和协议里程碑转化为清晰易懂的周更社区总结。
      • 与商务（Business）及开发（Dev）团队建立内部 SLA（服务等级协议），确保在敏感技术更新或变动引发社区无端猜测之前，社区团队能第一时间拿到官方统一口径。
    • 阶段 2 关键绩效指标（KPIs）：
      • 响应延迟： 在主要的技术支持频道中，确保针对已验证用户咨询的平均响应时间在 15 分钟以内。同时，随着工作流自动化工具的成熟，打通一条清晰的演进路径，在未来将该响应时间压缩至 5 分钟以内。
      • 舆情稳定性： 确保社区在面临核心技术里程碑时，重复性的焦虑言论出现率呈可测性下降，该指标将通过每周向委员会提交的量化情绪报告进行核验。
    🔹 阶段 3：区域枢纽整合与人才认可（第 3 个月及以后）

    该阶段聚焦于长期生态的可持续性发展、扩大 Qubic 的全球化触达深度，并凸显核心贡献者 de 价值。

    • 核心目标：
      • 为长期服务的志愿本地化贡献者建立一套正式的非货币性荣誉与价值矩阵（包括定制化特殊头衔、专属个人资料勋章/Badges，以及直接对接开发者的核心沟通通道）。
      • 规范和标准化全球区域子社区的日常加入与培训套件，确保全球社群在价值观及核心信息传递上保持绝对统一与合规。
    • 阶段 3 关键绩效指标（KPIs）：
      • 区域社区整合度： 成功将至少 [X] 个本地化社区枢纽平稳过渡并纳入统一的全球运营框架中。
      • 认可部署透明度： 确保非货币性特殊荣誉里程碑的部署达成率达 100%，且每月在 GitHub/论坛上进行透明公开的进度追踪。

    📈 供投资者审阅的执行 KPI 数据看板

    在我的领导下，社区团队的所有表现都将通过纯粹客观、可量化的核心指标进行持续性审计：

    指标类别 审计初始基准 (Baseline) 中期预定目标 (Target) 数据核验与追踪方法

    团队工作动力
    (5为最佳状态 / 1为最差状态)    		 4.1 / 5 (卓越) 持续稳定在 4.0 / 5 以上 每月团队定向匿名调查
    工具完备度与技术权限
    (5为最佳状态 / 1为最差状态)    		 2.62 / 5 (处于危机) 成功跨越并稳定在 4.0+ / 5 官方后台管理权限定期技术审计
    核心信息清晰度 暂无结构化机制 确保 0 发生高摩擦信息断层 官方全频道每周言论情绪深度分析

    正式确立社区负责人（Community Lead）这一职位，并不仅仅是一项开支，而是对 Qubic 最核心的防守资产——“社区”——进行的一笔极具战略意义的深远投资。该策略能从根本上确保我们在持续规模化扩张过程中的运营安全性、结构透明度以及专业的人才阶梯管理。

    在将本提案正式移交社区及委员会投票表决之前，我随时热烈欢迎并期待各位在下方提出宝贵的意见、质询与深入见解。

    感谢大家对 Qubic 始终如一的信任与奉献。
