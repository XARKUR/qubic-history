## **Strategic PR & Press Investment**

During the 12/01/2025 - 02/01/2026 term, Marketing funded \$6,800 toward a 90-day PR and press engagement towards the total amount of \$19,200. The remaining balance is reflected in the current proposal.

This funding covers the initial activation phase of a full-service PR program, including onboarding, strategy development, media list construction, and the launch of weekly editorial pitching cycles for 30 days, with the option to move forward and apply \$6800 already paid (in 12/1 - 02/01 proposal) towards the total package price of \$19,200, which will reduce the remaining 60-day commitment to \$12,400 as reflected in the marketing budget.

The decision to proceed with this PR and press partner followed a structured diligence process that included interviews conducted with five or more PR agencies, outlets, and independent specialists; comparative evaluation of deliverables, timelines, costs, and projected outcomes; internal discussions with team members; and a strategic assessment of how the engagement positions Qubic within AI, advanced compute, and decentralized technology narratives.

This investment was made proactively to elevate Qubic's presence in higher-level AI, technology, and crypto conversations; support long-term credibility; and establish earned media momentum that compounds beyond a single term.

## **Scope Expansion Since Last Proposal**

Since the approval of the previous marketing proposal, Qubic has been operating without a dedicated Operations Lead, without an approved Ambassador Program, and with increasing demand for coordinated execution across teams.

To prevent execution gaps and stalled initiatives, marketing absorbed several responsibilities previously managed by operations.

Marketing now functions as a cross-functional execution hub, ensuring alignment between science, ecosystem, community, content, visibility, and external outreach.

This shift was reactive but necessary and has proven effective based on measurable results.

## **Proof of Impact: Coordinated Execution**

To evaluate the impact of coordinated execution versus fragmented individual efforts, Marketing partnered with community ambassadors to run a one-day, coordinated social sprint on X.

Within a 24-hour period, this collective effort resulted in an approximate 300% increase in organic impressions and engagement. During this sprint, Qubic trended on X and achieved several notable outcomes, including coverage on CoinMarketCal featuring one of our AMAs, recognition as a Top 10 Crypto Coin based on social interaction, placement as a Top 8 AI Coin by LunarCrush, and advancement into the Top 5 AI coins by the end of the week.

These results reflect the meaningful contribution of ambassadors who rallied together—many voluntarily and without active compensation—to support Qubic’s visibility and momentum. The sprint demonstrated what is possible when the community aligns around a shared objective and executes in a coordinated way.

The insights gained from this initiative confirmed that cohesive, structured collaboration materially outperforms unstructured individual posting. As a result, Marketing is strategizing ways to help the Ambassador Program evolve into a more focused, impact-driven model that emphasizes coordination, quality control, and measurable outcomes, while continuing to recognize and value the efforts of community members who contribute to Qubic’s growth.

## **Team Structure Improvements**

To improve reliability and reduce execution risk, the team structure has been refined.

Automation and web development have been separated into two dedicated specialist roles, each with set deliverables. Roles are now prioritized based on confirmed availability and adherence to deadlines. Video production is finalized with a dedicated creator delivering five on-brand videos per month. Press and PR are handled by a full-service agency, with internal strategic direction to ensure message consistency.

This revised structure reduces bottlenecks and improves accountability.
