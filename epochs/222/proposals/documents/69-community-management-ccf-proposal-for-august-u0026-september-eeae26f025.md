## 本提案的范围是什么？

本提案旨在为社区团队在8月和9月期间继续领取薪资提供资金支持。

## 👥 什么是社区管理？

社区管理团队负责管理 Qubic 的 Discord 服务器和 Telegram 频道，并为多个区域性和多语言社群提供支持。社区管理作为协议开发、治理与社区参与者之间的稳定层。它还为生态系统中的其他工作组提供支持。核心团队由指定的版主和社区支持人员组成，同时也依靠志愿者。团队总人数超过20人。

**他们的职责包括：**

<br/>🤝 欢迎并引导新成员
<br/>🛡️ 执行社区规则
<br/>💬 促进建设性讨论
<br/>🎓 提供内部培训
<br/>🔧 协助其他工作组解决问题

## 🔍 过去两个月资金的使用情况 ##

由于市场状况和价格波动，社区团队采用了其动态薪酬模型，以保持在预算范围之内。尽管做出了这些调整，Discord 和 Telegram 上的运营仍持续不间断，团队全力专注于核心的版主管理、治理支持和社区安全工作。

这段时期的资金使社区管理团队得以在整个 Qubic 生态系统中维持一个安全、公平且充满活力的环境，同时也让我们得以重启一些直接惠及社区的关键举措。其中包括推出了一份技术性较低、更易理解的每周通讯，以拉近与日常用户的距离，同时创建了一个专门的意见与反馈渠道，该渠道反响热烈，已经收集到许多高质量、可行动的建议。此外，在其他工作组负责人的宝贵合作与支持下，我们成功重新整理并激活了官方常见问题解答（FAQ）。

最终，除了日常的版主管理、政策执行和技术支持工作之外，我们的团队将全力致力于帮助 Qubic 在其社区内促进更深入的理解和积极的教育，以确保生态系统保持一致且信息充分。

## 👥 What Is Community Management?

The Community Management Team moderates the Qubic Discord server and Telegram channels and supports several regional and multilingual hubs. Community Management acts as a stabilizing layer between protocol development, governance and community participants. It also brings support to other workgroups in the ecosystem. While the core team is composed of designated moderators and community support, it also relies on volunteers. Together they make up a team of more than 20 members.

**Their role include:**

<br/>🤝 Welcoming and onboarding new members
<br/>🛡️ Enforcing community rules
<br/>💬 Promoting constructive discussions
<br/>🎓 Providing internal training
<br/>🔧 Enhancing problem resolution among other workgroups

## 🔍 How We Used Past Funding during the last 2 months ##

Due to market conditions and price fluctuations, the Community Team applied its dynamic compensation model to remain within the allocated budget. Despite these adjustments, operations on Discord and Telegram continued uninterrupted, with a full focus on core moderation, governance support, and community safety.

Funding during this period enabled the Community Management Team to maintain a safe, fair, and engaging environment across the Qubic ecosystem, while allowing us to relaunch key initiatives that directly benefit the community. This included the introduction of a less technical, more accessible weekly newsletter to bring us closer to our daily users, alongside the creation of a dedicated ideas and feedback channel, which has received an excellent reception and is already yielding high-level, actionable insights. Furthermore, we have successfully reactivated the official FAQ by structuring critical information from across the ecosystem, with the valuable cooperation and support of other workgroup leads.

Ultimately, beyond our day-to-day work in moderation, policy enforcement, and technical support, our team is fully committed to helping Qubic foster a deeper understanding and active education within its community, ensuring an aligned and well-informed ecosystem.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> We apply a **dynamic salary model** based on price tiers. A 3-level salary system, subject to modifications, adjusts compensation based on Qubic price. If the price drops below set thresholds, salaries are reduced accordingly. If the price rises again, salaries are restored to higher levels. This model aligns compensation with market performance. It helps manage budget during downturns and rewards during growth. This model has already been applied in practice and allows the Community Team to remain operational without requiring emergency budget revisions.

<br/><br/>Monthly salary expenses were reduced from **$16,000** to **$11,000**; this represented approximately a **30% reduction** under current PA conditions. As of March, some moderators transitioned from full-time to part-time and following the Quorum's acceptance of Jg as Community Lead, the current monthly salary expense stands at $9,350.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> <br/>For over a year, we've been regularly hosting weekly fun events for the community. The server has approximately 60,000 members, with about 0.03-0.17% of the total server size actively participating. Events included weekly game nights, quiz nights, and occasional events based on larger events around the project. Events and the allocated budget have been discontinued.

<br/><br/>The community team will be taking a break from events to make a fresh start. Some malicious practices have been detected by a group of people who are only interested in exploiting the project without adding value in other aspects. We highly value genuine support and participation, so this break will help us filter out such behavior. We plan to resume these events later in the year and are even open to new ideas and types of events.
