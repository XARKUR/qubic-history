# Q-Stream Proposal  
**A Weekly Live Streaming Initiative to Expand Qubic’s Reach**

---

## Project Overview
We propose a 3-month pilot program for **Q-Stream** — a professionally produced, weekly 1.5-2hour live stream co-hosted by **Vibra Finance** and **Revo**, featuring guest speakers, audience participation, giveaways, and serious researched content that will add value to Qubic online representation.   
The program will serve as both an **onboarding funnel** for new users and a consistent **engagement driver** for the existing Qubic community.

---

## Funding Request
**1.5B Qubic / month × 3 months**  
- Equal split between Vibra Finance and Revo.  
- **100M-500 Qubic/month** allocated for viewer giveaways (If needed) and engagement incentives that will widen our reach. We will pay out on results.   
- Additional budget enables higher production value, wider promotion, and increased reach through targeted marketing.

---

## Objective
- Increase Qubic visibility beyond its current echo-chamber *No disrespect intended*.  
- Onboard new users, developers, and investors through accessible, engaging content.  
- Establish **Q-Stream** as the #1 trusted source for Qubic live discussions and updates.  

---

## Target Market
- Global crypto investors, with focus on **US, UK, and Europe** but with a plan for Chinese Market (and other markets) too as we develop the channel.  
- Both Qubic holders and non-holders, particularly small-to-mid-cap crypto enthusiasts mainly + some high net worth.  
- Developers seeking innovative blockchain platforms.
